# About me (and this website)

Thank you for visiting my website!

My name is Sean Sullivan, and I work as a freelance software developer. Most of my professional experience is using ReactJS/TypeScript, but I have worked full-stack using a variety of languages. My favorite languages to program with are TypeScript and Rust.

If you are looking to contact me for a business opportunity, please reach out to me using the contacts in the sidebar. I will try to respond as soon as I can.

## Projects

I usually maintain a few ongoing projects at any given time, and I like to write about my experiences here. Feel free to dig through my archive or check out my automated RSS feed here on this website, or check out the source code on GitHub.

Projects may take the form of websites, mobile apps, or whatever I might be interested in at the time. My list of ongoing projects often includes at least one game since, for me, game development is a fun way to express creative energy. This process is still new to me, but I hope to build technology with an impact.

Current projects include Betabuds, Psychic Chess, Tomecraft, this website. Look out for more soon!

## snen.dev

This website was made using the React 18 release candidate and leverages concurrent rendering to maximum effect. It is built and served using Deno, meaning it works without a framework. Learn more about why and how I did that here.

## Media

I post updates about this website and other interesting things I stumble upon on my Twitter. This website also hosts an RSS feed for all of my posts. If there’s a subscription format or feed you would like me to support, feel free to reach out.

I also try to spend some of my free time streaming on Twitch. This is generally free time, so I’m often either building open source technology or just playing video games and having fun. If you join, be kind and respectful.

I hope to use these channels to teach others and learn more myself, and to connect with others who like to build.
