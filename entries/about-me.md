# Welcome to snen.dev!

Thank you for visiting my website!

My name is Sean Sullivan, and I work as a freelance software developer. Most of my professional experience is using ReactJS/TypeScript, but I have worked full-stack using a variety of languages. My favorite programming languages are TypeScript and Rust. I co-founded [BetaBuds](https://www.betabuds.co/), and I have previously worked at [Kensho](https://kensho.com/) and [CadActive](https://cadactive.com/).

If you are looking to contact me for a business opportunity, please reach out to me using the contacts in the sidebar. I will try to respond as soon as I can.

## snen.dev

This website was made using the React 18 release candidate and aims to leverage concurrent rendering to its fullest. It is built and served using Deno, meaning it works without a framework. Learn more about why and how I did that [here](/reading/snen-dot-dev.md).
